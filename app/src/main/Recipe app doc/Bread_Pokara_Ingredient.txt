Bread_Pakora Ingredients:

4 slices Bread
2 Boiled Ptatoes(approx.200gm)
3 tablespoon Oil
4 medium size Chopped Onion
2 medium size Chopped Tomato
2 medium size Chopped Green Chili
1/2 tablespoon Cumin Powder
1/2 tablespoon Red Chili Powder
2 pinches Garam Masala Powder
A pinch of Baking Soda
Water(1/2 cup + 3 tablespoons) or as needed
1 cup Gram Flour (besan/chickpea flour)
1 tablespoon Rice Flour(optional)
Tomato Ketchup
Salt to taste
